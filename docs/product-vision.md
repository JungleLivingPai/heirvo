# Product Vision

**One sentence:** DVD Rescue is a Windows app that recovers damaged home-video DVDs and outputs a playable, optionally AI-enhanced MP4 — built for people who are scared, not technical.

## Who it's for

A 35-year-old non-technical person who just discovered their wedding/childhood/parent-funeral DVD won't play. They are emotional, not informed, and one bad UX away from giving up.

## The promise

> Even if your disc is too damaged to play, we'll recover everything we can — and you'll never lose progress.

## Three rules that override every other decision

1. **Never fail the whole job because of one unreadable sector.** Always produce a playable output, even if partial.
2. **Always be resumable.** Pulling the disc, crashing, rebooting — recovery picks up exactly where it left off, including merging reads from different drives or sessions.
3. **Speak human.** No "UDF", "ISO9660", "VOB", "IFO", "sector LBA". Translate everything. *"We found 47 minutes of video across 3 chapters."*

## Anti-goals

- Not a Blu-ray ripper. Not a piracy tool. (Don't touch CSS decryption beyond what makes sense for personal recovery.)
- Not a video editor.
- Not cross-platform v1. Windows only.
- Not cloud. 100% local processing is a feature, not a limitation.
- Not subscription. One-time license.
